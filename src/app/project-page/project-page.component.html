CASE STUDY - RESIDENTIAL SPACES

Meganite’s solid surface solutions have evolved from being mere functional home additions to leading the design of residential spaces. The owner of this home has chosen our award-winning M007 – Mt. Carrara marble-inspired look to transform and enhance their space, with the material’s combination of practical solid surface properties and elegant designer appeal. Meganite’s essential properties offer the opportunity to gain a superior, luxury home lifestyle.

THE BENEFITS OF THE MEGANITE SOLUTION

“ The choice of Mt. Carrara from our Movement Series offered the homeowner the luxury of marble design without the hassle of real marble. “

Meganite is a non-porous material which limits bacteria growth and makes the hygienic kitchen and bathroom spaces easy to clean. It also boasts an NSF/ANSI Standard 51 certification for food contact which made it a safe and ideal material for the kitchen countertops and the food preparation area. The material’s Thermoformable properties enabled the crafting of marble inspired furniture such as the vinyl record player, which fits perfectly with the other materials utilized in the home’s interior design. The durable and easy to clean material was also perfect for high traffic areas like the kitchen island which require extra care.
