Delivering A Positive First Impression with Meganite

First impression matters. Every piece of furniture will contribute to guests’ impressions, but countertops are one of the most significant elements.

From the reception area and the product display to the bathrooms and the boardroom, guests might visit multiple spaces. Selecting the right material for countertops will help business make a positive progess.

Versatile And Hygienic
for High-traffic Space

Excelling in high traffic areas, Meganite Solid Surface is durable and hygienic enough to withstand the frequent touches by the ongoing flow of guests and staff.

Save Intangible Costs of Maintenance

Maintaining the utmost hygiene standard and reducing the risk of bacterial growth is crucial. With the nonporous and seamless advantages, Meganite is easy to clean and maitain which save intangible maintencance cost for owers in the long run.

Endless Design Possibilities

Taking advantage of solid surface thermoforming abilities, you can create striking curves with inconspicuous joints for commercial design plans. Meganite can fulfill all your design needs with unlimited shapes and color combinations.

A Sustainable Choice
